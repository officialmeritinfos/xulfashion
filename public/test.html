What's Next?

Now that you’re part of the Xulfashion family, here’s how to make the most of your new account:

  • Complete Your Profile: Start by filling out your profile. A complete profile builds trust and helps improve your visibility on the platform.
  • Get Verified: Verification is optional but necessary to receive payments directly into your online account. This step ensures secure transactions for you and your customers.
  • Create a Store: Click on the Store menu, select “Initialize Store,” and fill out the necessary details. This step sets up your dedicated storefront, allowing you to manage your products and engage with customers directly.
  • Create an Ad/Listing: Boost your visibility by creating ads. These listings will appear in our marketplace, helping you reach more potential customers.
  • Add Products: Navigate to the Store page, click on catalog management, and start adding categories and products. Note that categories must be added before products, although a default category is provided.
  • Setup Storefront: Customize your storefront to suit your brand. Once you’re on the Store page, you’ll find various options to set up and enhance your store.
  • Setup Payout Account: Ensure you’ve set up your payout account so you can receive payments. This step is crucial for managing your earnings.
  • Share Store & Catalog: Promote your store and catalog by sharing them across your networks. The more you share, the greater your reach.
